# 📘 Lesson Notes – Types of Verbs and Forms of Verbs (Grade 7–9)

---

# 🌟 What is a Verb?

A **verb** is a word that shows:

* an action
* a state
* or an occurrence

### Examples:

* She **writes** neatly.
* They **play** football.
* He **is** happy.

---

# 📚 Types of Verbs Based on Their Past Forms

Verbs can be divided into:

1. **Regular Verbs**
2. **Irregular Verbs**

---

# 1️⃣ Regular Verbs

Regular verbs form their Past Tense and Past Participle by adding:

* **-d**
* **-ed**
* **-ied**

to the root verb.

---

## Examples

| Root Verb | Past    | Past Participle |
| --------- | ------- | --------------- |
| play      | played  | played          |
| walk      | walked  | walked          |
| clean     | cleaned | cleaned         |
| watch     | watched | watched         |
| study     | studied | studied         |

---

## Sentences

### Present:

* I **play** cricket.

### Past:

* I **played** cricket yesterday.

### Present Perfect:

* I have **played** cricket before.

---

# 2️⃣ Irregular Verbs

Irregular verbs do not follow a fixed rule when forming the Past Tense and Past Participle.

Their forms must be learned and memorized.

---

## Examples

| Root Verb | Past  | Past Participle |
| --------- | ----- | --------------- |
| go        | went  | gone            |
| eat       | ate   | eaten           |
| write     | wrote | written         |
| see       | saw   | seen            |
| speak     | spoke | spoken          |
| drive     | drove | driven          |

---

## Sentences

### Present:

* She **writes** well.

### Past:

* She **wrote** a letter.

### Present Perfect:

* She has **written** a letter.

---

# 📚 Forms of Verbs

Every verb can appear in different forms.

The five most common forms are:

1. Root Form
2. S Form
3. ING Form
4. Past Form
5. Past Participle Form

---

# 1️⃣ Root Form (Base Form)

This is the original form of the verb.

### Examples:

* play
* eat
* write
* study
* go

### Sentences:

* I **play** football.
* They **eat** lunch at school.

---

# 2️⃣ S Form

Used mainly with:

* He
* She
* It

in the Simple Present Tense.

---

## Rules

### Add "s"

* play → plays
* eat → eats

### Add "es"

* watch → watches
* go → goes

### Change y → ies

* study → studies
* carry → carries

---

## Examples

* He **plays** cricket.
* She **studies** English.
* It **goes** fast.

---

# 3️⃣ ING Form

Used in Continuous Tenses.

---

## Examples

| Root  | ING Form |
| ----- | -------- |
| play  | playing  |
| eat   | eating   |
| write | writing  |
| run   | running  |
| swim  | swimming |

---

## Sentences

* She is **playing** football.
* They are **studying** grammar.

---

# 4️⃣ Past Form

Used to talk about actions completed in the past.

---

## Examples

| Root  | Past   |
| ----- | ------ |
| play  | played |
| walk  | walked |
| go    | went   |
| eat   | ate    |
| write | wrote  |

---

## Sentences

* I **played** football yesterday.
* She **wrote** a letter.

---

# 5️⃣ Past Participle Form

Used with helping verbs such as:

* has
* have
* had
* is
* was

---

## Examples

| Root  | Past Participle |
| ----- | --------------- |
| play  | played          |
| go    | gone            |
| eat   | eaten           |
| write | written         |
| see   | seen            |

---

## Sentences

* They have **played** the match.
* She has **written** a letter.
* We have **seen** that movie.

# 🎯 Easy Trick

| Form            | Example Sentence              |
| --------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Root            | I play football.              |
| S Form          | He plays football.            |
| ING Form        | He is playing football.       |
| Past            | He played football yesterday. |
| Past Participle | He has played football.       |

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 1

2. They are ______ (study) for the exam.

3. He ______ (go) to Bengaluru yesterday.

4. I have ______ (eat) my lunch.

5. She ______ (write) a letter every week.

1. plays

2. studying

3. went

4. eaten

5. writes
