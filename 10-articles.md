# 📘 Articles

## 🌟 What are Articles?

Articles are special adjectives used before nouns.

They help us know whether we are talking about:

- something general
  or
- something specific

There are **3 articles** in English:

- **a**
- **an**
- **the**

---

# 📚 Types of Articles

| Type                | Articles |
| ------------------- | -------- |
| Indefinite Articles | a, an    |
| Definite Article    | the      |

---

# 1️⃣ Indefinite Articles – _A_ and _An_

We use **a** and **an** with singular countable nouns when talking about something general or not specific.

---

## ✅ Use “A”

Use **a** before words that begin with a **consonant sound**.

### Examples:

- a boy
- a car
- a university
- a teacher

### Sentences:

- I saw **a dog** in the street.
- She is **a doctor**.

---

## ✅ Use “An”

Use **an** before words that begin with a **vowel sound**.

### Examples:

- an apple
- an umbrella
- an hour
- an honest man

### Sentences:

- He ate **an orange**.
- It took **an hour** to finish.

---

# ⚠️ Important Note

We look at the **sound**, not just the spelling.

| Word       | Correct Article | Reason     |
| ---------- | --------------- | ---------- |
| university | a university    | “yu” sound |
| hour       | an hour         | silent “h” |

---

# 2️⃣ Definite Article – _The_

We use **the** when talking about:

- something specific
- something already known

### Examples:

- the sun
- the principal
- the book on the table

### Sentences:

- Please close **the door**.
- **The moon** looks beautiful tonight.

---

# 📝 When to Use “The”

### Use “the”:

✅ For unique things
→ the Earth, the Taj Mahal

✅ When both speaker and listener know the thing
→ the classroom, the teacher

✅ Before rivers, oceans, mountain ranges
→ the Ganga, the Himalayas

---

# 🚫 When Articles Are Not Used

We usually do NOT use articles:

- before most proper nouns
  → Rahul, India

- before languages and subjects
  → English, Mathematics

- before plural/general nouns
  → Dogs are loyal animals.

---

# 🎯 Quick Comparison

| Article | Usage           | Example  |
| ------- | --------------- | -------- |
| a       | consonant sound | a cat    |
| an      | vowel sound     | an egg   |
| the     | specific noun   | the book |

# ✏️ Practice Exercise

Fill in the blanks with **a, an,** or **the**.

1. She bought **\_\_** umbrella.
2. I saw **\_\_** tiger in the zoo.
3. **\_\_** sun rises in the east.
4. He is **\_\_** honest man.
5. We visited **\_\_** Red Fort last year.

---

# ✅ Answers

1. an
2. a
3. The
4. an
5. the
