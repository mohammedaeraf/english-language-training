# 📘 Introduction to Grammar – Parts of Speech

## 🌟 What is Grammar?

**Grammar** is the set of rules that helps us speak and write a language correctly.

Grammar teaches us:

- How to make correct sentences
- How to use words properly
- How to communicate clearly

### Example:

✅ She is going to school.
❌ She going school.

---

# 🧩 What are Parts of Speech?

Words in English are divided into different groups according to their use in a sentence. These groups are called **Parts of Speech**.

There are **8 main Parts of Speech** in English Grammar.

---

# 📚 The 8 Parts of Speech

| No. | Part of Speech | Function                              | Example           |
| --- | -------------- | ------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| 1   | Noun           | Names a person, place, thing, or idea | boy, school       |
| 2   | Pronoun        | Replaces a noun                       | he, she, they     |
| 3   | Adjective      | Describes a noun/pronoun              | beautiful, tall   |
| 4   | Verb           | Shows action or state                 | run, eat, is      |
| 5   | Adverb         | Describes a verb/adjective/adverb     | quickly, very     |
| 6   | Preposition    | Shows relation                        | in, on, under     |
| 7   | Conjunction    | Joins words or sentences              | and, but, because |
| 8   | Interjection   | Shows sudden feeling/emotion          | wow!, oh!         |

---

# 1️⃣ Noun

A noun is the name of a person, place, thing, or idea.

### Examples:

- teacher
- Mumbai
- book
- honesty

### Sentence:

- The **teacher** entered the class.

---

# 2️⃣ Pronoun

A pronoun is used instead of a noun.

### Examples:

- he
- she
- they
- it

### Sentence:

- Riya is absent because **she** is sick.

---

# 3️⃣ Adjective

An adjective describes a noun or pronoun.

### Examples:

- tall
- red
- clever

### Sentence:

- It is a **beautiful** garden.

---

# 4️⃣ Verb

A verb shows action or state of being.

### Examples:

- run
- write
- sing
- is

### Sentence:

- The boys **played** football.

---

# 5️⃣ Adverb

An adverb describes a verb, adjective, or another adverb.

### Examples:

- slowly
- very
- loudly

### Sentence:

- She sang **beautifully**.

---

# 6️⃣ Preposition

A preposition shows the relationship between words.

### Examples:

- in
- on
- under
- beside

### Sentence:

- The bag is **under** the table.

---

# 7️⃣ Conjunction

A conjunction joins words or sentences.

### Examples:

- and
- but
- because

### Sentence:

- I wanted to play, **but** it was raining.

---

# 8️⃣ Interjection

An interjection expresses sudden emotion or feeling.

### Examples:

- Wow!
- Oh!
- Hurray!

### Sentence:

- **Hurray!** We won the match.

---

# 🎯 Example Sentence Analysis

### Sentence:

**The smart boy ran quickly into the classroom.**

| Word      | Part of Speech |
| --------- | -------------- |
| The       | Article        |
| smart     | Adjective      |
| boy       | Noun           |
| ran       | Verb           |
| quickly   | Adverb         |
| into      | Preposition    |
| classroom | Noun           |

---

# 📝 Easy Memory Trick

> **Nouns name**
> **Pronouns replace**
> **Adjectives describe**
> **Verbs act**
> **Adverbs explain**
> **Prepositions relate**
> **Conjunctions join**
> **Interjections express feelings**
