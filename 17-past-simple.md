# 📘 Lesson Notes – Past Simple Tense (Grade 7–9)

---

# 🌟 What is the Past Simple Tense?

The **Past Simple Tense** is used to talk about:

* Actions that **started and finished in the past**
* Completed events
* Past habits
* Events that happened at a specific time in the past

---

## ✨ Examples

* She studied English yesterday.
* They played football last Sunday.
* I visited Mysuru last month.
* We watched a movie yesterday.

---

# 📚 Structure of Past Simple Tense

## ✅ Positive Sentences

### Structure

> **Subject + Past Form of Verb (V2)**

### Examples

| Subject | Sentence                    |
| ------- | --------------------------- |
| I       | I played cricket yesterday. |
| We      | We visited Bengaluru.       |
| You     | You completed the project.  |
| They    | They won the match.         |
| He      | He wrote a letter.          |
| She     | She cooked dinner.          |
| It      | It rained heavily.          |

---

# 📝 Verb Forms Used

The **Past Simple Tense** uses the **Past Form (V2)** of the verb.

### Regular Verbs

| Root  | Past    |
| ----- | ------- |
| play  | played  |
| walk  | walked  |
| study | studied |
| clean | cleaned |
| visit | visited |

### Irregular Verbs

| Root  | Past   |
| ----- | ------ |
| go    | went   |
| eat   | ate    |
| write | wrote  |
| see   | saw    |
| speak | spoke  |
| drive | drove  |
| come  | came   |
| take  | took   |
| buy   | bought |
| give  | gave   |

---

# ❌ Negative Sentences

### Structure

> **Subject + did not (didn't) + Base Verb (V1)**

⚠️ After **did**, always use the **base form** of the verb.

### Examples

* I did not play cricket.
* She did not study English.
* They did not go to school.
* He did not write a letter.

---

# ❓ Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> **Did + Subject + Base Verb (V1)?**

### Examples

* Did you play football?
* Did she study English?
* Did they win the match?
* Did he write the letter?

---

# ❓ Wh Questions

Wh Questions begin with:

* What
* Where
* When
* Why
* Who
* Which
* How

### Structure

> **Wh Word + did + Subject + Base Verb (V1)?**

---

## Examples

### What

* What did she write?
* What did they buy?

### Where

* Where did you go?
* Where did Rahul study?

### When

* When did they arrive?
* When did you finish your homework?

### Why

* Why did she leave early?
* Why did he call you?

### How

* How did they travel?
* How did you solve the problem?

### Who

* Who won the match?
* Who visited you yesterday?

---

# ❓ Negative Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> **Didn't + Subject + Base Verb (V1)?**

### Examples

* Didn't he study yesterday?
* Didn't they play football?
* Didn't she write the assignment?

---

# ❓ Negative Wh Questions

### Structure

> **Wh Word + didn't + Subject + Base Verb (V1)?**

### Examples

* Why didn't he study?
* Why didn't they come to school?
* What didn't she understand?
* Where didn't they go?

---

# 📚 Uses of Past Simple Tense

---

## 1️⃣ Completed Actions

* She finished her homework yesterday.
* We visited the museum.

---

## 2️⃣ Specific Time in the Past

* They arrived at 9 a.m.
* Rahul called me last night.

---

## 3️⃣ Past Habits

* We played cricket every evening.
* My grandfather walked every morning.

---

## 4️⃣ Sequence of Events

* She woke up, brushed her teeth, and left for school.
* We reached the station, bought tickets, and boarded the train.

---

# 📝 Common Time Expressions

Past Simple Tense is commonly used with:

* yesterday
* last night
* last week
* last month
* last year
* ago
* in 2023
* this morning (if finished)
* yesterday evening

### Examples

* I met him **yesterday**.
* She visited Delhi **last month**.
* We completed the work **two days ago**.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Incorrect:

* She did not went to school.

### Correct:

* She did not go to school.

---

### Incorrect:

* Did he wrote a letter?

### Correct:

* Did he write a letter?

---

### Incorrect:

* They didn't played football.

### Correct:

* They didn't play football.

---

### Incorrect:

* He goed to school.

### Correct:

* He went to school.

---

# 📝 Past Simple vs Present Perfect

| Past Simple                     | Present Perfect           |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Used with a specific past time. | Time is not specified.    |
| I visited Delhi last year.      | I have visited Delhi.     |
| She wrote a letter yesterday.   | She has written a letter. |
| They won the match last week.   | They have won the match.  |

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 1

Fill in the blanks with the correct **Past Simple** form of the verb.

1. She ______ (study) English yesterday.

2. They ______ (play) football last Sunday.

3. I ______ (visit) Mysuru last month.

4. We ______ (watch) a movie yesterday.

5. He ______ (write) a letter.

---

# ✅ Answers

1. studied

2. played

3. visited

4. watched

5. wrote

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 2

Convert the following into:

1. Negative Sentence
2. Yes/No Question
3. Wh Question

### Example

**She studied English yesterday.**

* Negative: She did not study English yesterday.
* Yes/No Question: Did she study English yesterday?
* Wh Question: What did she study yesterday?

---

### Questions

1. They played football yesterday.

2. We visited Bengaluru last week.

3. He wrote a letter yesterday.

4. She cooked dinner last night.

5. The students completed the project yesterday.

---

# ✅ Answers

## 1. They played football yesterday.

* Negative: They did not play football yesterday.
* Yes/No Question: Did they play football yesterday?
* Wh Question: What did they play yesterday?

---

## 2. We visited Bengaluru last week.

* Negative: We did not visit Bengaluru last week.
* Yes/No Question: Did we visit Bengaluru last week?
* Wh Question: Where did we visit last week?

---

## 3. He wrote a letter yesterday.

* Negative: He did not write a letter yesterday.
* Yes/No Question: Did he write a letter yesterday?
* Wh Question: What did he write yesterday?

---

## 4. She cooked dinner last night.

* Negative: She did not cook dinner last night.
* Yes/No Question: Did she cook dinner last night?
* Wh Question: What did she cook last night?

---

## 5. The students completed the project yesterday.

* Negative: The students did not complete the project yesterday.
* Yes/No Question: Did the students complete the project yesterday?
* Wh Question: What did the students complete yesterday?

---

# 🎯 Quick Formula Summary

| Type                     | Structure                                    |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------- |
| Positive                 | Subject + Verb (Past Form V2)                |
| Negative                 | Subject + did not + Base Verb (V1)           |
| Yes/No Question          | Did + Subject + Base Verb (V1)?              |
| Wh Question              | Wh Word + did + Subject + Base Verb (V1)?    |
| Negative Yes/No Question | Didn't + Subject + Base Verb (V1)?           |
| Negative Wh Question     | Wh Word + didn't + Subject + Base Verb (V1)? |
