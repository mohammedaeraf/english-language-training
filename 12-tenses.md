# 📘 Tenses – Introduction

## 🌟 What are Tenses?

**Tenses** tell us the time of an action.

They show whether an action happens:

- now
- in the past
- or in the future

Tenses are formed using verbs.

---

# 📚 The Three Main Tenses

| Tense         | Time              | Example         |
| ------------- | ----------------- | --------------- |
| Present Tense | happening now     | She writes.     |
| Past Tense    | already happened  | She wrote.      |
| Future Tense  | will happen later | She will write. |

---

# 1️⃣ Present Tense

The **Present Tense** shows:

- actions happening now
- regular habits
- general truths

### Examples:

- I play cricket.
- She studies every day.
- The sun rises in the east.

---

## 🔹 Simple Present Structure

### Subject + Verb (base form)

### Examples:

- They play football.
- He plays football.

---

# 2️⃣ Past Tense

The **Past Tense** shows actions that already happened.

### Examples:

- I visited Bengaluru yesterday.
- She completed her homework.

---

## 🔹 Simple Past Structure

### Subject + Verb (past form)

### Examples:

- They watched a movie.
- He played cricket.

---

# 3️⃣ Future Tense

The **Future Tense** shows actions that will happen later.

### Examples:

- I will call you tomorrow.
- They will travel next week.

---

## 🔹 Simple Future Structure

### Subject + will + Verb

### Examples:

- She will sing.
- We will study tonight.

---

# 📝 Verb Changes in Tenses

| Base Verb | Present    | Past   | Future    |
| --------- | ---------- | ------ | --------- |
| play      | play/plays | played | will play |
| go        | go/goes    | went   | will go   |
| eat       | eat/eats   | ate    | will eat  |

---

# ⚠️ Subject–Verb Agreement

| Subject       | Verb  |
| ------------- | ----- |
| He/She/It     | plays |
| I/We/You/They | play  |

### Examples:

- She plays badminton.
- They play badminton.

---

# 🎯 Easy Trick

| Clue Word            | Likely Tense |
| -------------------- | ------------ |
| every day, always    | Present      |
| yesterday, last week | Past         |
| tomorrow, next year  | Future       |
