## 💡 Practice Exercise

Please create five sentences using the same verb in different forms. Do it for at least 10 verbs.

**Verb: Write**

* I **write** neatly.
* He **writes** neatly.
* She is **writing** a letter.
* She **wrote** a letter yesterday.
* She has **written** a letter.

This will help you understand how verb forms change with tense and usage.
