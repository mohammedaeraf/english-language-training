# 📘 Lesson Notes – Present Continuous Tense 

---

# 🌟 What is the Present Continuous Tense?

The **Present Continuous Tense** is used to talk about:

* Actions happening **right now**
* Temporary actions happening around the present time
* Planned future actions

It is also called the **Present Progressive Tense**.

---

## ✨ Examples

* She is studying now.
* They are playing football.
* I am writing a letter.
* We are learning English.

---

# 📚 Structure of Present Continuous Tense

## ✅ Positive Sentences

### Structure

> Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing

### Examples

| Subject | Sentence                   |
| ------- | -------------------------- |
| I       | I am studying.             |
| He      | He is reading a book.      |
| She     | She is cooking dinner.     |
| We      | We are learning English.   |
| They    | They are playing football. |

---

# 📝 Helping Verbs

| Subject       | Helping Verb |
| ------------- | ------------ |
| I             | am           |
| He            | is           |
| She           | is           |
| It            | is           |
| Singular Noun | is           |
| We            | are          |
| You           | are          |
| They          | are          |
| Plural Noun   | are          |

---

# ❌ Negative Sentences

### Structure

> Subject + am/is/are + not + Verb-ing

### Examples

* I am not sleeping.
* He is not studying.
* They are not playing cricket.
* We are not watching television.

---

# ❓ Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> Am/Is/Are + Subject + Verb-ing?

### Examples

* Am I speaking clearly?
* Is she studying?
* Are they playing football?
* Are we going to school?

---

# ❓ Wh Questions

Wh Questions begin with:

* What
* Where
* When
* Why
* Who
* How

### Structure

> Wh Word + am/is/are + Subject + Verb-ing?

---

## Examples

### What

* What is she studying?
* What are they doing?

### Where

* Where are you going?
* Where is he working?

### When

* When are they arriving?

### Why

* Why is she crying?
* Why are they laughing?

### How

* How are you travelling?

### Who

* Who is speaking?

---

# ❓ Negative Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> Isn't / Aren't + Subject + Verb-ing?

### Examples

* Isn't he studying?
* Aren't they playing football?
* Isn't she coming today?

---

# ❓ Negative Wh Questions

### Structure

> Wh Word + isn't/aren't + Subject + Verb-ing?

### Examples

* Why isn't he studying?
* Why aren't they going to school?
* What isn't she doing?

---

# 📝 Rules for Adding ING

---

## Rule 1: Add "ing"

| Verb | ING Form |
| ---- | -------- |
| play | playing  |
| read | reading  |
| cook | cooking  |

---

## Rule 2: Drop Silent "e"

| Verb  | ING Form |
| ----- | -------- |
| write | writing  |
| make  | making   |
| drive | driving  |

---

## Rule 3: Double the Final Consonant

| Verb | ING Form |
| ---- | -------- |
| run  | running  |
| swim | swimming |
| sit  | sitting  |

# ⏰ Common Time Expressions

Present Continuous is often used with:

* now
* right now
* at the moment
* currently
* today

### Examples

* She is studying **now**.
* We are working **at the moment**.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Incorrect:

* She studying now.

### Correct:

* She is studying now.

---

### Incorrect:

* They is playing football.

### Correct:

* They are playing football.

---

### Incorrect:

* Is she study now?

### Correct:

* Is she studying now?

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 1

Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb.

1. She ______ ______ (read) a book.

2. They ______ ______ (play) football.

3. I ______ ______ (write) a letter.

4. We ______ ______ (learn) English.

5. He ______ ______ (watch) television.

---

# ✅ Answers

1. is reading

2. are playing

3. am writing

4. are learning

5. is watching
