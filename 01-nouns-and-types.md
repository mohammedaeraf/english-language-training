# 📘 Nouns and Their Types – Quick Reference (Grade 7–9)

## 🌟 What is a Noun?

A **noun** is the name of a:

- **Person**
- **Place**
- **Thing**
- **Animal**
- **Idea/Feeling**

### Examples:

- Person → teacher, doctor, Aisha
- Place → school, park, Mumbai
- Thing → book, table, phone
- Animal → cat, elephant
- Idea/Feeling → honesty, happiness, courage

---

# 📚 Types of Nouns

---

## 1️⃣ Proper Noun

A **Proper Noun** is the special name of a particular person, place, or thing.
It always begins with a **capital letter**.

### Examples:

- Rohan
- India
- Taj Mahal
- Monday

### Sentences:

- **Riya** lives in **Delhi**.
- We visited the **Red Fort**.

---

## 2️⃣ Common Noun

A **Common Noun** is a general name for a person, place, animal, or thing.

### Examples:

- boy
- city
- school
- river

### Sentences:

- The **boy** is playing.
- Our **school** is large.

---

## 3️⃣ Collective Noun

A **Collective Noun** names a group of people, animals, or things.

### Examples:

- team
- class
- flock
- bunch

### Sentences:

- The **team** won the match.
- A **flock** of birds flew away.

---

## 4️⃣ Abstract Noun

An **Abstract Noun** is the name of a feeling, quality, idea, or state that we cannot see or touch.

### Examples:

- honesty
- love
- bravery
- happiness

### Sentences:

- **Honesty** is the best policy.
- Her **kindness** helped everyone.

---

## 5️⃣ Material Noun

A **Material Noun** names a material or substance from which things are made.

### Examples:

- gold
- wood
- cotton
- silver

### Sentences:

- This ring is made of **gold**.
- The chair is made of **wood**.

---

# 📝 Quick Comparison Table

| Type            | Meaning              | Examples     |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ------------ |
| Proper Noun     | Specific name        | Ahmed, India |
| Common Noun     | General name         | boy, city    |
| Collective Noun | Group name           | team, herd   |
| Abstract Noun   | Feeling/idea/quality | honesty, joy |
| Material Noun   | Substance/material   | gold, iron   |

---

# 🎯 Easy Trick to Remember

| Question               | Type of Noun    |
| ---------------------- | --------------- |
| Special name?          | Proper Noun     |
| General name?          | Common Noun     |
| Group name?            | Collective Noun |
| Feeling or quality?    | Abstract Noun   |
| Material or substance? | Material Noun   |

# ✏️ Practice Exercise

Identify the type of noun:

1. The **army** marched forward.
2. **Rahul** loves cricket.
3. Her **beauty** impressed everyone.
4. The table is made of **steel**.
5. The **girl** is reading.

### Answers:

1. Collective Noun
2. Proper Noun
3. Abstract Noun
4. Material Noun
5. Common Noun
