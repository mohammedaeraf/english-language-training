# ✏️ Assignment: Simple Present Tense (50 Fill-in-the-Blanks)

## 📘 Instructions

Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb given in brackets.

Remember:

Positive Sentences - 
Use the base form of the verb with I, We, You, They.
Add s/es with He, She, It.

Negative Sentences - 
* **I, We, You, They** → do / do not
* **He, She, It** → does / does not
* After **do/does**, use the **base form** of the verb.
