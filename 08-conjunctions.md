# 📘 Conjunctions

## 🌟 What is a Conjunction?

A **conjunction** is a word that joins:

- words
- phrases
- or sentences

Conjunctions help make sentences smoother and more meaningful.

---

# ✨ Examples

- Rahul **and** Ahmed are friends.
- I was tired, **but** I finished my homework.
- She stayed home **because** she was sick.

---

# 📚 Types of Conjunctions

---

## 1️⃣ Coordinating Conjunctions

These join words or groups of equal importance.

### Common Coordinating Conjunctions:

- and
- but
- or
- so
- yet
- for
- nor

### Sentences:

- I bought apples **and** oranges.
- He is poor, **but** honest.
- Hurry up, **or** you will miss the bus.

---

## 2️⃣ Subordinating Conjunctions

These join a dependent clause to an independent clause.

### Common Examples:

- because
- although
- if
- when
- while
- since

### Sentences:

- She stayed home **because** she was ill.
- **Although** it was raining, we played outside.
- I will call you **when** I arrive.

---

## 3️⃣ Correlative Conjunctions

These work in pairs.

### Common Examples:

- either...or
- neither...nor
- both...and
- not only...but also

### Sentences:

- **Either** study hard **or** fail the exam.
- **Both** Ali **and** Zoya attended the event.
- **Neither** the teacher **nor** the students were late.

---

# 📝 FANBOYS Rule

The seven coordinating conjunctions can be remembered using:

## 🎯 FANBOYS

| Letter | Conjunction |
| ------ | ----------- |
| F      | For         |
| A      | And         |
| N      | Nor         |
| B      | But         |
| O      | Or          |
| Y      | Yet         |
| S      | So          |

---

# ⚠️ Important Notes

| Conjunction | Use              |
| ----------- | ---------------- |
| and         | adds information |
| but         | shows contrast   |
| or          | shows choice     |
| because     | gives reason     |
| so          | shows result     |

---

# ✨ Sentence Joining Examples

### Without conjunction:

- I was hungry. I ate food.

### With conjunction:

- I was hungry, **so** I ate food.

# ✏️ Practice Exercise

Choose the correct conjunction.

1. I wanted to play, **\_\_** it started raining.
   _(but / because)_

2. She was absent **\_\_** she was sick.
   _(because / or)_

3. You can have tea **\_\_** coffee.
   _(or / but)_

4. Rahul **\_\_** Ahmed are classmates.
   _(and / yet)_

5. **\_\_** he worked hard, he failed the test.
   _(Although / So)_

---

# ✅ Answers

1. but
2. because
3. or
4. and
5. Although
