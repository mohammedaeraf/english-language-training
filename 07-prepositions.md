# 📘 Prepositions

## 🌟 What is a Preposition?

A **preposition** is a word that shows the relationship between:

- a noun (or pronoun)
  and
- another word in the sentence.

Prepositions often show:

- place
- time
- direction
- position

---

# ✨ Examples

- The book is **on** the table.
- We will meet **after** lunch.
- The cat ran **under** the bed.

---

# 📚 Types of Prepositions

---

## 1️⃣ Prepositions of Place

Show the position or location of something.

### Common Examples:

- in
- on
- under
- beside
- behind
- between

### Sentences:

- The keys are **on** the table.
- The dog is sleeping **under** the chair.
- She sat **between** her friends.

---

## 2️⃣ Prepositions of Time

Show time.

### Common Examples:

- at
- on
- in
- before
- after

### Sentences:

- The class starts **at** 9 a.m.
- We celebrate Independence Day **on** August 15.
- He was born **in** 2010.

---

## 3️⃣ Prepositions of Direction/Movement

Show movement from one place to another.

### Common Examples:

- to
- into
- towards
- across
- through

### Sentences:

- She walked **into** the room.
- The boy ran **across** the road.
- We are going **to** school.

---

# 📝 Common Prepositions

| Place  | Time   | Movement |
| ------ | ------ | -------- |
| in     | at     | to       |
| on     | on     | into     |
| under  | before | across   |
| beside | after  | through  |
| behind | in     | towards  |

---

# ⚠️ Important Usage

| Preposition | Usage                 | Example                |
| ----------- | --------------------- | ---------------------- |
| in          | months, years, cities | in July, in India      |
| on          | days, dates, surfaces | on Monday, on the wall |
| at          | exact time/place      | at 5 p.m., at home     |

---

# 🎯 Easy Trick

A preposition usually comes before a noun or pronoun.

### Examples:

- under **the table**
- near **him**
- behind **the door**
