# 📘 Interjections

## 🌟 What is an Interjection?

An **interjection** is a word or short phrase that expresses:

* sudden emotion
* feeling
* reaction

Interjections are usually followed by:

* an exclamation mark (!)
  or sometimes
* a comma (,)

---

# ✨ Examples

* **Wow!** That painting is beautiful.
* **Oh no!** I forgot my homework.
* **Hurray!** We won the match.

---

# 📚 Common Interjections and Their Feelings

| Interjection | Feeling/Emotion     |
| ------------ | ------------------- |
| Wow!         | Surprise/Admiration |
| Hurray!      | Joy                 |
| Alas!        | Sorrow              |
| Ouch!        | Pain                |
| Oh!          | Surprise            |
| Bravo!       | Praise              |
| Hey!         | Calling attention   |
| Oops!        | Mistake             |

---

# 📝 Examples in Sentences

### Joy

* **Hurray!** Our team won.

### Surprise

* **Wow!** This place is amazing.

### Pain

* **Ouch!** That hurt my hand.

### Sadness

* **Alas!** The bird is dead.

### Attention

* **Hey!** Wait for me.

---

# ⚠️ Important Points

* Interjections are often used in informal speech and writing.
* They are not grammatically connected to the rest of the sentence.
* They help express emotions strongly.

---

# ✨ Punctuation with Interjections

| Example               | Punctuation      |
| --------------------- | ---------------- |
| Wow! This is great.   | Exclamation mark |
| Oh, I understand now. | Comma            |

---

# 🎯 Remember

Interjections usually come:

* at the beginning of a sentence
* and express feelings instantly
