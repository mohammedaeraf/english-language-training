# 📘 Lesson Notes – Present Perfect Tense (Grade 7–9)

---

# 🌟 What is the Present Perfect Tense?

The **Present Perfect Tense** is used to talk about:

- Actions that have been completed recently
- Actions that happened in the past but are connected to the present
- Life experiences
- Actions whose exact time is not mentioned

---

## ✨ Examples

- She has completed her homework.
- They have won the match.
- I have eaten my lunch.
- We have visited Mysuru.

---

# 📚 Structure of Present Perfect Tense

## ✅ Positive Sentences

### Structure

> Subject + has/have + Past Participle (V3)

### Examples

| Subject | Sentence                       |
| ------- | ------------------------------ |
| I       | I have finished my work.       |
| We      | We have completed the project. |
| You     | You have done well.            |
| They    | They have arrived.             |
| He      | He has written a letter.       |
| She     | She has cooked dinner.         |
| It      | It has stopped raining.        |

# ❌ Negative Sentences

### Structure

> Subject + has/have + not + Past Participle

### Examples

- I have not finished my work.
- She has not completed the homework.
- They have not arrived yet.
- We have not seen that movie.

---

# ❓ Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> Has/Have + Subject + Past Participle?

### Examples

- Have you finished your work?
- Have they arrived?
- Has she completed the homework?
- Has he written the letter?

---

# ❓ Wh Questions

Wh Questions begin with:

- What
- Where
- When
- Why
- Who
- How

### Structure

> Wh Word + has/have + Subject + Past Participle?

---

## Examples

### What

- What have you done?
- What has she written?

### Where

- Where have they gone?
- Where has he travelled?

### When

- When have you completed the project?

### Why

- Why have they left early?
- Why has she called you?

### How

- How have you solved the problem?

### Who

- Who has completed the assignment?

---

# ❓ Negative Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> Hasn't/Haven't + Subject + Past Participle?

### Examples

- Hasn't he finished the homework?
- Haven't they arrived yet?
- Hasn't she submitted the project?

---

# ❓ Negative Wh Questions

### Structure

> Wh Word + hasn't/haven't + Subject + Past Participle?

### Examples

- Why hasn't he completed the work?
- Why haven't they arrived yet?
- What hasn't she finished?

---

# 📚 Uses of Present Perfect Tense

---

## 1️⃣ Recently Completed Actions

- She has finished her homework.
- We have cleaned the classroom.

---

## 2️⃣ Life Experiences

- I have visited Delhi.
- They have travelled by airplane.

---

## 3️⃣ Actions with Present Results

- He has lost his keys.
- The students have completed the project.

---

## 4️⃣ Actions with Unspecified Time

- She has watched that movie.
- We have met the principal.

---

# 📝 Common Time Expressions

Present Perfect Tense is often used with:

- already
- just
- yet
- ever
- never
- recently
- lately
- so far

### Examples

- I have just finished my lunch.
- She has already completed the assignment.
- Have you finished yet?
- We have never visited Kashmir.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Incorrect:

- She has went to school.

### Correct:

- She has gone to school.

---

### Incorrect:

- They have ate lunch.

### Correct:

- They have eaten lunch.

---

### Incorrect:

- Has she finished her work.

### Correct:

- Has she finished her work?

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 1

Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb.

1. She **\_\_** **\_\_** (complete) her homework.

2. They **\_\_** **\_\_** (win) the match.

3. I **\_\_** **\_\_** (eat) my lunch.

4. We **\_\_** **\_\_** (visit) Mysuru.

5. He **\_\_** **\_\_** (write) a letter.

---

# ✅ Answers

1. has completed

2. have won

3. have eaten

4. have visited

5. has written

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 2

Convert the following into:

1. Negative Sentence
2. Yes/No Question
3. Wh Question

### Example

**She has completed her homework.**

- Negative: She has not completed her homework.
- Yes/No Question: Has she completed her homework?
- Wh Question: What has she completed?

---

### Questions

# ✅ Answers

## 1. They have won the match.

- Negative: They have not won the match.
- Yes/No Question: Have they won the match?
- Wh Question: What have they won?

---

## 2. We have visited Bengaluru.

- Negative: We have not visited Bengaluru.
- Yes/No Question: Have we visited Bengaluru?
- Wh Question: Where have we visited?

---

## 3. He has written a letter.

- Negative: He has not written a letter.
- Yes/No Question: Has he written a letter?
- Wh Question: What has he written?

---

## 4. She has cooked dinner.

- Negative: She has not cooked dinner.
- Yes/No Question: Has she cooked dinner?
- Wh Question: What has she cooked?

---

## 5. The students have completed the project.

- Negative: The students have not completed the project.
- Yes/No Question: Have the students completed the project?
- Wh Question: What have the students completed?
