# ✏️ Fill in the Blanks – Countable & Uncountable Nouns

Use the correct word from the brackets.

---

## 📝 Exercise 01

1. There are ______ apples in the basket.
   *(many / much)*

2. We need ______ water to make juice.
   *(some / many)*

3. She has ______ books on her table.
   *(much / several)*

4. There is very ______ milk left in the fridge.
   *(little / few)*

5. I bought ______ bananas from the market.
   *(a little / a few)*

6. We do not have ______ sugar at home.
   *(many / much)*

7. The teacher gave us ______ information about the project.
   *(many / some)*

8. There are ______ students absent today.
   *(few / little)*

9. Please add ______ rice to the bowl.
   *(a little / a few)*

10. My brother has ______ friends in Bengaluru.
    *(much / many)*
