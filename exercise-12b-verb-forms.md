# ✏️ Exercise – Using Different Forms of the Same Verb

## Instructions

For each verb given below, create **five sentences** using the following forms:

1. Root Form
2. S Form
3. ING Form
4. Past Form
5. Past Participle Form

### Example

**Verb: Write**

* I write neatly.
* He writes neatly.
* She is writing a letter.
* She wrote a letter yesterday.
* She has written a letter.

# ✅ Sample Solutions

## 1. Play

* I play cricket every evening.
* He plays cricket every evening.
* They are playing cricket now.
* We played cricket yesterday.
* We have played cricket before.

---

## 2. Go

* I go to school every day.
* She goes to school every day.
* They are going to school now.
* He went to school yesterday.
* He has gone to school.

---

## 3. Eat

* I eat fruits daily.
* She eats fruits daily.
* They are eating lunch now.
* He ate lunch at noon.
* He has eaten his lunch.
