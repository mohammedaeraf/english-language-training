# ✅ Answers – Extra Challenge

For each sentence, the Positive Sentence is already given. The task is to write the Negative Sentence and the Yes/No Question.

---

## 1. She studies English.

### Positive:

* She studies English.

### Negative:

* She does not study English.

### Yes/No Question:

* Does she study English?
