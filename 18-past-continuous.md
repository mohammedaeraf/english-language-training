# 📘 Lesson Notes – Past Continuous Tense (Grade 7–9)

---

# 🌟 What is the Past Continuous Tense?

The **Past Continuous Tense** is used to talk about:

* An action that **was happening at a particular time in the past**.
* Two actions happening **at the same time in the past**.
* A longer action that was interrupted by another action.

---

## ✨ Examples

* She was studying at 8 p.m.
* They were playing football yesterday evening.
* I was reading a book when my friend called.
* We were watching television at that time.

---

# 📚 Structure of Past Continuous Tense

## ✅ Positive Sentences

### Structure

> **Subject + was/were + Verb-ing**

### Examples

| Subject | Sentence                    |
| ------- | --------------------------- |
| I       | I was reading a book.       |
| He      | He was studying English.    |
| She     | She was cooking dinner.     |
| It      | It was raining.             |
| We      | We were learning grammar.   |
| You     | You were writing notes.     |
| They    | They were playing football. |

---

# 📝 Helping Verbs

| Subject       | Helping Verb |
| ------------- | ------------ |
| I             | was          |
| He            | was          |
| She           | was          |
| It            | was          |
| Singular Noun | was          |
| We            | were         |
| You           | were         |
| They          | were         |
| Plural Noun   | were         |

---

# 📝 Rules for Adding **-ing**

## Rule 1: Add **-ing**

| Verb | ING Form |
| ---- | -------- |
| play | playing  |
| read | reading  |
| cook | cooking  |

---

## Rule 2: Drop the silent **e**

| Verb  | ING Form |
| ----- | -------- |
| write | writing  |
| make  | making   |
| drive | driving  |

---

## Rule 3: Double the final consonant

| Verb | ING Form |
| ---- | -------- |
| run  | running  |
| swim | swimming |
| sit  | sitting  |

---

# ❌ Negative Sentences

### Structure

> **Subject + was/were + not + Verb-ing**

### Examples

* She was not studying.
* They were not playing football.
* I was not sleeping.
* We were not waiting.

---

# ❓ Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> **Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing?**

### Examples

* Was she studying?
* Were they playing football?
* Was he sleeping?
* Were you waiting?

---

# ❓ Wh Questions

Wh Questions begin with:

* What
* Where
* When
* Why
* Who
* How

### Structure

> **Wh Word + was/were + Subject + Verb-ing?**

---

## Examples

### What

* What was she reading?
* What were they doing?

### Where

* Where were you going?
* Where was he working?

### When

* When were they travelling?

### Why

* Why was she crying?
* Why were they laughing?

### How

* How were you travelling?

### Who

* Who was speaking?

---

# ❓ Negative Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> **Wasn't/Weren't + Subject + Verb-ing?**

### Examples

* Wasn't he studying?
* Weren't they playing football?
* Wasn't she working?

---

# ❓ Negative Wh Questions

### Structure

> **Wh Word + wasn't/weren't + Subject + Verb-ing?**

### Examples

* Why wasn't he studying?
* Why weren't they attending school?
* What wasn't she doing?

---

# 📚 Uses of Past Continuous Tense

---

## 1️⃣ Action Happening at a Specific Time in the Past

* She was studying at 8 p.m.
* We were having dinner at that time.

---

## 2️⃣ Two Actions Happening Simultaneously

* While I was reading, my sister was writing.
* They were singing while we were dancing.

---

## 3️⃣ Interrupted Action

A longer action was interrupted by a shorter action (usually in the Past Simple Tense).

### Examples

* I was sleeping when the phone rang.
* They were playing football when it started to rain.
* She was cooking when the guests arrived.

---

# 📝 Common Time Expressions

Past Continuous is commonly used with:

* at 8 p.m.
* yesterday evening
* last night
* while
* when
* all day
* all morning
* at that time

### Examples

* He was studying **last night**.
* They were playing football **when I arrived**.
* We were travelling **at that time**.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Incorrect:

* She was studying English.

### Correct:

* She was studying English.

---

### Incorrect:

* They was playing football.

### Correct:

* They were playing football.

---

### Incorrect:

* Was she study English?

### Correct:

* Was she studying English?

---

### Incorrect:

* He were reading a book.

### Correct:

* He was reading a book.

---

# 📝 Past Simple vs Past Continuous

| Past Simple                   | Past Continuous                              |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Describes a completed action. | Describes an action in progress in the past. |
| She cooked dinner.            | She was cooking dinner.                      |
| They played football.         | They were playing football.                  |
| He wrote a letter.            | He was writing a letter.                     |

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 1

Fill in the blanks with the correct **Past Continuous** form of the verb.

1. She ______ ______ (study) at 8 p.m.

2. They ______ ______ (play) football yesterday evening.

3. I ______ ______ (read) a book.

4. We ______ ______ (watch) television.

5. He ______ ______ (write) a letter.

---

# ✅ Answers

1. was studying

2. were playing

3. was reading

4. were watching

5. was writing
