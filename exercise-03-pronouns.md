# ✏️ Fill in the Blanks – Pronouns

Choose the correct pronoun from the brackets.

---

## 📝 Exercise

1. Riya is my friend. ______ is very kind.
   *(She / He)*

2. My brother and I are going to the park. ______ will play cricket.
   *(We / They)*

3. I saw Rahul yesterday. I spoke to ______.
   *(him / her)*

4. This book belongs to me. It is ______.
   *(mine / yours)*

5. The children completed the project by ______.
   *(themselves / ourselves)*

6. ______ is knocking at the door?
   *(Who / Which)*

7. I have two bags. ______ one is blue.
   *(These / This)*

8. My parents love me, and I love ______ too.
   *(them / us)*

9. ______ forgot to bring the notebook to class.
   *(Someone / Herself)*

10. The cat is hungry. Please give ______ some milk.
    *(it / them)*
