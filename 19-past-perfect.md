# 📘 Lesson Notes – Past Perfect Tense (Grade 7–9)

---

# 🌟 What is the Past Perfect Tense?

The **Past Perfect Tense** is used to talk about:

- An action that **was completed before another action in the past**.
- The **earlier of two past actions**.
- A completed action before a specific time in the past.

It is often called the **"Past Before the Past"** tense.

---

## ✨ Examples

- She had completed her homework before dinner.
- They had left before we arrived.
- I had eaten breakfast before I went to school.
- We had finished the project by Friday.

---

# 📚 Structure of Past Perfect Tense

## ✅ Positive Sentences

### Structure

> **Subject + had + Past Participle (V3)**

### Examples

| Subject | Sentence                      |
| ------- | ----------------------------- |
| I       | I had finished my work.       |
| We      | We had completed the project. |
| You     | You had done your homework.   |
| They    | They had arrived early.       |
| He      | He had written a letter.      |
| She     | She had cooked dinner.        |
| It      | It had stopped raining.       |

---

# 📝 Helping Verb

Unlike many other tenses, **all subjects use "had".**

| Subject       | Helping Verb |
| ------------- | ------------ |
| I             | had          |
| We            | had          |
| You           | had          |
| They          | had          |
| He            | had          |
| She           | had          |
| It            | had          |
| Singular Noun | had          |
| Plural Noun   | had          |

---

# 📚 Verb Forms Used

The Past Perfect Tense uses the **Past Participle (V3)** of the verb.

| Root Verb | Past (V2) | Past Participle (V3) |
| --------- | --------- | -------------------- |
| play      | played    | played               |
| study     | studied   | studied              |
| write     | wrote     | written              |
| eat       | ate       | eaten                |
| go        | went      | gone                 |
| see       | saw       | seen                 |
| do        | did       | done                 |
| speak     | spoke     | spoken               |
| drive     | drove     | driven               |
| take      | took      | taken                |

---

# ❌ Negative Sentences

### Structure

> **Subject + had + not + Past Participle (V3)**

### Examples

- She had not completed her homework.
- They had not arrived before us.
- I had not seen the movie.
- We had not finished the work.

---

# ❓ Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> **Had + Subject + Past Participle (V3)?**

### Examples

- Had you finished your work?
- Had they arrived before us?
- Had she completed her homework?
- Had he written the letter?

---

# ❓ Wh Questions

Wh Questions begin with:

- What
- Where
- When
- Why
- Who
- How

### Structure

> **Wh Word + had + Subject + Past Participle (V3)?**

---

## Examples

### What

- What had she written?
- What had they bought?

### Where

- Where had you gone?
- Where had Rahul travelled?

### When

- When had they arrived?

### Why

- Why had she left early?
- Why had he called you?

### How

- How had they solved the problem?

### Who

- Who had completed the assignment?

---

# ❓ Negative Yes/No Questions

### Structure

> **Hadn't + Subject + Past Participle (V3)?**

### Examples

- Hadn't he finished the homework?
- Hadn't they arrived on time?
- Hadn't she submitted the assignment?

---

# ❓ Negative Wh Questions

### Structure

> **Wh Word + hadn't + Subject + Past Participle (V3)?**

### Examples

- Why hadn't he completed the work?
- Why hadn't they arrived earlier?
- What hadn't she understood?

---

# 📚 Uses of Past Perfect Tense

---

## 1️⃣ An Action Completed Before Another Past Action

- She had finished her homework before she watched TV.
- They had eaten dinner before the guests arrived.

---

## 2️⃣ The Earlier of Two Past Actions

- When I reached the station, the train had already left.
- By the time we arrived, the movie had started.

---

## 3️⃣ A Completed Action Before a Specific Time

- We had completed the project by Friday.
- He had left before 8 a.m.

---

# 📝 Common Time Expressions

Past Perfect is often used with:

- before
- after
- by
- already
- just
- when
- by the time

### Examples

- She had already finished her homework.
- We had left before the rain started.
- They had completed the work by Friday.
- By the time I arrived, the bus had left.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Incorrect:

- She had went to school.

### Correct:

- She had gone to school.

---

### Incorrect:

- They had ate dinner.

### Correct:

- They had eaten dinner.

---

### Incorrect:

- Had she finished her homework.

### Correct:

- Had she finished her homework?

---

### Incorrect:

- He had wrote a letter.

### Correct:

- He had written a letter.

---

# 📝 Past Simple vs Past Perfect

| Past Simple                             | Past Perfect                                          |
| --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Shows one completed action in the past. | Shows an action completed before another past action. |
| She finished her homework.              | She had finished her homework before dinner.          |
| They arrived at 8 p.m.                  | They had arrived before we reached.                   |
| I ate breakfast.                        | I had eaten breakfast before I left home.             |

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 1

Fill in the blanks with the correct **Past Perfect** form of the verb.

1. She **\_\_** **\_\_** (complete) her homework before dinner.

2. They **\_\_** **\_\_** (leave) before we arrived.

3. I **\_\_** **\_\_** (eat) breakfast before school.

4. We **\_\_** **\_\_** (finish) the project by Friday.

5. He **\_\_** **\_\_** (write) a letter before going home.

---

# ✅ Answers

1. had completed

2. had left

3. had eaten

4. had finished

5. had written

# ✏️ Practice Exercise 2

Convert the following into:

1. Negative Sentence
2. Yes/No Question
3. Wh Question

### Example

**She had completed her homework before dinner.**

- Negative: She had not completed her homework before dinner.
- Yes/No Question: Had she completed her homework before dinner?
- Wh Question: What had she completed before dinner?

---

### Questions

1. They had left before we arrived.

2. We had visited Bengaluru before moving to Mysuru.

3. He had written a letter before leaving.

4. She had cooked dinner before the guests arrived.

5. The students had completed the project before the deadline.

---

# ✅ Answers

## 1. They had left before we arrived.

- Negative: They had not left before we arrived.
- Yes/No Question: Had they left before we arrived?
- Wh Question: When had they left?

---

## 2. We had visited Bengaluru before moving to Mysuru.

- Negative: We had not visited Bengaluru before moving to Mysuru.
- Yes/No Question: Had we visited Bengaluru before moving to Mysuru?
- Wh Question: Where had we visited before moving to Mysuru?

---

## 3. He had written a letter before leaving.

- Negative: He had not written a letter before leaving.
- Yes/No Question: Had he written a letter before leaving?
- Wh Question: What had he written before leaving?

---

## 4. She had cooked dinner before the guests arrived.

- Negative: She had not cooked dinner before the guests arrived.
- Yes/No Question: Had she cooked dinner before the guests arrived?
- Wh Question: What had she cooked before the guests arrived?

---

## 5. The students had completed the project before the deadline.

- Negative: The students had not completed the project before the deadline.
- Yes/No Question: Had the students completed the project before the deadline?
- Wh Question: What had the students completed before the deadline?

# 🎯 Quick Formula Summary

| Yes/No Question          | Had + Subject + Past Participle (V3)?              |
| Wh Question              | Wh Word + had + Subject + Past Participle (V3)?    |
| Negative Yes/No Question | Hadn't + Subject + Past Participle (V3)?           |
| Negative Wh Question     | Wh Word + hadn't + Subject + Past Participle (V3)? |
