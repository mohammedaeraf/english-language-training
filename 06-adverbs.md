# 📘 Adverbs 

## 🌟 What is an Adverb?

An **adverb** is a word that describes:

* a **verb**
* an **adjective**
* or another **adverb**

An adverb tells us:

* **How?**
* **When?**
* **Where?**
* **How often?**
* **To what extent?**

---

# ✨ Examples

* She sings **beautifully**. *(How?)*
* We will leave **tomorrow**. *(When?)*
* The children are playing **outside**. *(Where?)*
* He **always** arrives early. *(How often?)*

---

# 📚 Types of Adverbs

---

## 1️⃣ Adverb of Manner

Tells **how** something happens.

### Examples:

* slowly
* carefully
* loudly
* beautifully

### Sentences:

* She danced **gracefully**.
* The teacher spoke **softly**.

---

## 2️⃣ Adverb of Time

Tells **when** something happens.

### Examples:

* today
* yesterday
* soon
* now

### Sentences:

* I will call you **tomorrow**.
* She arrived **late**.

---

## 3️⃣ Adverb of Place

Tells **where** something happens.

### Examples:

* here
* there
* outside
* everywhere

### Sentences:

* Please sit **here**.
* The children are waiting **outside**.

---

## 4️⃣ Adverb of Frequency

Tells **how often** something happens.

### Examples:

* always
* never
* often
* sometimes

### Sentences:

* He **always** helps others.
* We **rarely** watch TV.

---

## 5️⃣ Adverb of Degree

Tells **how much** or **to what extent**.

### Examples:

* very
* too
* almost
* quite

### Sentences:

* The water is **very** cold.
* She was **too** tired to walk.

---

# 📝 Position of Adverbs

Adverbs can appear:

* Before the verb
* After the verb
* At the beginning/end of a sentence

### Examples:

* She **quickly** finished her homework.
* He speaks English **fluently**.
* **Yesterday**, we visited the museum.

---

# ⚠️ Adjective vs Adverb

| Adjective              | Adverb                          |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Describes noun/pronoun | Describes verb/adjective/adverb |
| slow train             | runs slowly                     |
| beautiful girl         | sings beautifully               |

---

# 🎯 Easy Trick

Many adverbs end in **-ly**

| Adjective | Adverb    |
| --------- | --------- |
| quick     | quickly   |
| careful   | carefully |
| happy     | happily   |

But not all adverbs end in **-ly**:

* fast
* well
* hard
* often
