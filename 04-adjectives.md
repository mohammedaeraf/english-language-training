# 📘 Adjectives 

## 🌟 What is an Adjective?

An **adjective** is a word that describes or gives more information about a noun or pronoun.

It tells us:

- What kind?
- Which one?
- How many?

---

## ✨ Examples

- a **beautiful** flower
- a **tall** building
- **three** pencils
- a **red** car

In these examples, the highlighted words describe nouns.

---

# 📚 Types of Adjectives

---

## 1️⃣ Adjective of Quality

Describes the quality or nature of a person, place, or thing.

### Examples:

- honest
- brave
- beautiful
- clever

### Sentences:

- She is a **kind** girl.
- It was a **beautiful** painting.

---

## 2️⃣ Adjective of Quantity

Shows how much of something is present.

### Examples:

- some
- little
- much
- enough

### Sentences:

- We have **enough** food.
- There is **little** water in the bottle.

---

## 3️⃣ Adjective of Number

Shows number or position.

### Examples:

- one
- two
- first
- many

### Sentences:

- I bought **three** mangoes.
- He stood in the **first** row.

---

## 4️⃣ Demonstrative Adjective

Points out specific nouns.

### Examples:

- this
- that
- these
- those

### Sentences:

- **This** book is interesting.
- **Those** students are quiet.

---

## 5️⃣ Interrogative Adjective

Used with nouns to ask questions.

### Examples:

- which
- what
- whose

### Sentences:

- **Which** subject do you like?
- **Whose** bag is this?

---

## 6️⃣ Possessive Adjective

Shows ownership or possession.

### Examples:

- my
- your
- his
- her
- our
- their

### Sentences:

- This is **my** notebook.
- **Their** house is large.

---

# 📝 Quick Comparison Table

| Type          | Purpose            | Examples          |
| ------------- | ------------------ | ----------------- |
| Quality       | Describes quality  | beautiful, honest |
| Quantity      | Shows amount       | some, little      |
| Number        | Shows number/order | three, first      |
| Demonstrative | Points out nouns   | this, those       |
| Interrogative | Asks questions     | which, whose      |
| Possessive    | Shows ownership    | my, their         |

---

# 🎯 Remember

An adjective usually comes:

- Before a noun
  → a **small** house

- After verbs like _is, are, was_
  → The house is **small**.
