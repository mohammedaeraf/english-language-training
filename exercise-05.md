# ✏️ Exercise on Verbs (10 Questions)

Identify:

1. The **verb**
2. The **type of verb**

---

## 📝 Questions

1. The children played football in the evening.

2. She is a talented singer.

3. Rahul can solve difficult problems.

4. The baby slept peacefully.

5. They have completed the project.

6. The teacher wrote the answer on the board.

7. We were happy after the competition.

8. My brother drives carefully.

9. The birds flew across the lake.

10. She became angry after hearing the news.

---

# ✅ Answers

| No. | Verb           | Type of Verb             |
| --- | -------------- | ------------------------ |
| 1   | played         | Action Verb              |
| 2   | is             | Linking Verb             |
| 3   | can solve      | Helping Verb + Main Verb |
| 4   | slept          | Intransitive Verb        |
| 5   | have completed | Helping Verb + Main Verb |
| 6   | wrote          | Transitive Verb          |
| 7   | were           | Linking Verb             |
| 8   | drives         | Action Verb              |
| 9   | flew           | Intransitive Verb        |
| 10  | became         | Linking Verb             |
